Privacy Preserving Contact Tracing with Bluetooth Signals

Tested how Bluetooth Low Energy signals could be used for automated and privacy preserving contact tracing on Raspberry Pis. Ran experiments testing how distance, obstacles, and orientation affected the received signal strength indicator. Trained models using sklearn to use gyroscope and accelerometer data and bluetooth signal strength for accurate distance predictions.
